MyWallets allows investors to easily track the profit/loss of their cryptocurrencies using long and short term analyses. You can manually enter the number of cryptocurrencies you have or connect to the blockchain if you have a public key.

List of features offered by MyWallets:

. WALLETS FOLLOW-UP
Overview of the profits, investments and losses of each wallet.

. DETAILED WALLET ANALYSES WITH CHARTS
Long term analysis of the value of its wallets, profit/loss of each wallet and a comparison of the percentage of the amount invested/percentage of the wallet value.

. TRACKING THE CURRENT MARKET
A complete list of cryptocurrencies.
