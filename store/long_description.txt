MyWallets allows investors to easily track the profit/loss of their cryptocurrencies using long and short term analyses and setting up personalized alerts to react quickly to this highly volatile market. You can manually enter the number of cryptocurrencies you have
or connect to the blockchain if you have a public key.

List of features offered by MyWallets:

. WALLETS FOLLOW-UP
Overview of the profits, investments and losses of each wallet.

. DETAILED WALLET ANALYSES WITH CHARTS
Long term analysis of the value of its wallets, profit/loss of each wallet and a comparison of the percentage of the amount invested / percentage of the wallet value.

. ADDING ALERTS
Quick alert addition with a personalized note.

. TRACKING THE CURRENT MARKET
A complete list of cryptocurrencies.
